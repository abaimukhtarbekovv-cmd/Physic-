15. Biot-Savart Law (Integral Form) Definition/Main Principle:



Biot-Savart Law — dB = (μ₀/4π) (I dl × r‌)/r².



Permeability of Free Space (μ₀) — μ₀ = 4π×10⁻⁷ T·m/A.



Right-Hand Rule — For direction of dB: point thumb along I dl, fingers curl toward r‌, palm pushes in direction of dB.



Physical Meaning & Explanation: This is the fundamental law for calculating magnetic fields from steady currents. The field is perpendicular to both the current element and the displacement vector. The total field is the vector integral over the current path.



Relevant Formulas: B = ∫ dB = (μ₀I/4π) ∫ (dl × r‌)/r².



Example: Field on the axis of a circular loop of radius R: B = (μ₀ I R²) / (2(R² + x²)^{3/2}).
